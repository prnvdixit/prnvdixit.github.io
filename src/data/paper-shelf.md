## Attention Mechanisms

Neural Machine Translation by Jointly Learning to Align and Translate | https://arxiv.org/pdf/1409.0473 | The seminal paper that introduced the attention mechanism to neural machine translation, allowing models to focus on specific parts of the input sequence.

## Efficient Transformers

Efficient Transformers: A Survey | https://arxiv.org/pdf/1606.02909 | A comprehensive survey of various efficient transformer architectures, discussing techniques to reduce the complexity of the self-attention mechanism.
A Survey on Efficient Training of Transformers | https://arxiv.org/pdf/2302.01107 | This survey focuses on the latest advancements in training transformers efficiently, covering hardware-aware optimizations and algorithmic improvements.
FlashAttention: Fast and Memory-Efficient Exact Attention with IO-Awareness | https://arxiv.org/pdf/2205.14135 | Introduces FlashAttention, an IO-aware exact attention algorithm that significantly speeds up transformer training and inference by reducing memory accesses.
